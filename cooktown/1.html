In this story, you are Alexis, a young baker in need of a big break. You have to make the choices that influence Alexis's life, from which dog to adopt to which bakery you should buy.

Will Alexis become a world wide famous chef, or will all of the big dreams burn down in flames?

"Alexis, what have you gotten yourself into now?" Belle, your best friend asks. "Are you really going to start a career out of your little hobby? If this really happens, I can't promise to tell you what you want to hear. I'll tell you the truth."
