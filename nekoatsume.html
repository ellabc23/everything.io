🐾Neko Atsume🐾

Neko Atsume is a cute cat collecting game where you use silver and gold fish to buy goodies and food for your cats. This page will be my blog of some sorts with little tips and tricks.

Useful Tips

•Always do the daily reward. It is located in the news section of the game. Then, you press on the cat right of the password and enter it where it asks for it. You will recieve a small amount of silver and sometimes gold fish. Every five days that you enter the password, you will earn a Ritzy Bitz can for free!
